关于哥本哈根皮草

Kopenhagen Fur’s History

Kopenhagen Fur是世界上最大的毛皮拍卖及贸易中心。该公司由1.600名丹麦水貂养殖者共同拥有，并在1930年成立丹麦动物养殖者者协会。

Kopenhagen Fur is the world's largest fur auction house and trade center, founded the Danish Fur Breeders Association in 1930 and cooperatively owned by 1600 mink breeders.

自成立以来，哥本哈根皮草拥有明确的发展目标并伴随着企业创进意识，其整体目标是通过收集和分享知识来开发、优化和改进皮草生产，并采用先进的设备和最人性化的条件保障,这是在哥本哈根皮草的基础价值核心。

As always, Kopenhagen Fur embraces clear target and innovation conscious, its whole target is to develop and improve fur production by knowledge collection and sharing, as well as adapt advancing equipment and most humane security, which is the basic core value of Kopenhagen Fur.

随着我们不断发展，我们已成为皮草和时装行业最具创新精神的合作伙伴，并将持续深化和铸造具有北欧特色的皮草时尚创新孵化平台。

Kopenhagen Fur has became the most innovation partner in fur and fashion industry, and will continuously create and deepen the Nordic incubator platform of fur fashion.

我们的愿景是成为世界领先的可持续皮草之家

We want to be the world’s leading sustainable house of fur
